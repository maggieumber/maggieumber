Sound of Snow Falling

Synopsis

Sound of Snow Falling is a graphic novel done in a poetic documentary mode. In this unique work, the reader becomes a voyeur of the natural world, following a great horned owl family through the dead of winter. Extensively researched and expressively painted, Sound of Snow Falling is a triumph of the comics form. $22.95 | 104 pages | 6.8 × 8.5 inches | full color offset | flexibound
