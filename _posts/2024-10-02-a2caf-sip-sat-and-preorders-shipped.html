This Saturday, I'll be in the artist alley at A2CAF: Small + Indie Press. My first time tabling in Michigan I think?! I'll have advanced copies of Chrysanthemum Under the Waves, Rust Belt Review vol 6, Death Spark 2 and my usual assortment of graphic novels, zines, prints, cards and toys.

A2CAF: Small + Indie Press | Saturday, October 5, 2024
11 AM - 5 PM | Downtown Library

Ann Arbor Comic Arts Festival (A2CAF): Small + Indie Press is a one-day venture into the world of small press comics publishing. This event offers attendees the chance to meet comic artists and learn about the art of creating comics outside of a traditional publisher. Due to the content of many independently published books, this event is intended for a teen and adult audience. A2CAF: Small + Indie Press is created in partnership with Athenaeum Comic Art. Our friends at Vault of Midnight will be at A2CAF Small + Indie Press selling books by all our headliners for signings.

Thank you to everyone who picked up their advanced debut copy of Chrysanthemum Under the Waves last weekend at Cartoon Crossroads Columbus. I am so grateful to you all and to Jared Gardner and OSU Pop Culture Studies for having me as their special guest. We had a conversation which will later be on the CXC youtube channel.
