What a lineup! Come find me on Saturday among this incredible group of printers!

6th Annual CPG Publishers Fair
Chicago Cultural Center
78 E. Washington St
Sat. Oct. 12 11am-5pm

ALL are welcome to join us for the 6th Annual Chicago Printers Guild Publishers Fair on Saturday, October 12! This year our flagship event will be held in the beautiful Chicago Cultural Center, where more than 30 CPG members and printmakers will come together to share new work and conversation at our vibrant in-person marketplace.
