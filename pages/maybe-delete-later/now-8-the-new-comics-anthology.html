Now #8: The New Comics Anthology

Synopsis

The 2018 Eisner Award-nominated anthology series continues with another entirely self-contained collection of short comics genius! Now #8 features an original cover by Al Columbia (Pim & Francie) with all-new stories by Theo Ellsworth, Sami Alwani, E.S. Glenn, Veronika Muchitsch, Henry McCausland, Zuzu, Noah Van Sciver, Walt Holcombe, Maggie Umber, Tara Booth, and Nick Thorburn! A cross-section of brilliant cartooning and storytelling, spanning the globe, at the best price point in comics! $12.99 | 128 pages | 7.125 x 10.125 inches | full color

The way [Umber] draws her parents as having blank eyes behind their eyeglasses is almost as perfect as the way she draws herself: a crazy-haired curmudgeon with either a glint of mischief or a permanent scowl on her face. - Rob Clough

This is among the best volumes yet of Fantagraphics’ ongoing Now comics anthology. While this volume leans more than usual on established talent rather than on emerging cartoonists, every comic in this batch is worth reading. — Jesús, Goodreads
