About

I paint, print and program comics. I've published the graphic novels Sound of Snow Falling, Time Capsule and 270°. I'm at work on the upcoming semi-autobiographical horror collection The Man in the Blue Suit and baking humor graphic novel Bunworld. I'm Co-Founder of the alternative comics publishing label 2dcloud.

My comics have been published in Fantagraphics Now The New Comics Anthology #5 (The Daemon Lover), Bomb Magazine (There is Water), Spiralbound (The Rock), Anomaly 25 (The Tooth comics animation), Ninth Art Press's The Shirley Jackson Project (The Tooth). They have also been a part of the group shows ZINE NOT DEAD VIIII: A New Comics Reading (WE ARE HUNGRY!), zeitraumexit in Germany ("...and other stories") and The Learning Machine in Chicago (Plants and Animals).
