This Friday, 6/14 6-8pm, is the opening reception for Drawing Worlds: The art of comics and beyond. The exhibit runs through 7/3/2024 at A2AC in Ann Arbor, MI.

In this show, I'll have 6 large prints from Chrysanthemum Under the Waves, my 80 page zine Chrysanthemum: the Storyboards, and a video sharing my process for making the zine. Thank you to A2AC for exhibiting my work!
