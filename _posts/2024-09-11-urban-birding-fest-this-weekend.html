I printed a bunch of greeting cards for the Urban Birding Festival this weekend. This will be my first time EVER tabling at a birding fest!!!

The inaugural Urban Birding Festival will be held in and around Chicago on September 14-15, 2024—highlighting the extraordinary bird diversity present in the area with over 400 recorded species in the Chicago region. Our central hub for is:
Peggy Notebaert Nature Museum
2430 N Cannon Dr, Chicago, IL 60614
September 13-15 from 10-4 pm
Our Vendor Marketplace will be open on Saturday and Sunday 10-4 pm at Peggy Notebaert. And, we’ll have bird outings all across the city!

This weekend is also SPX (Small Press Expo) in Bethesda, Maryland. I won't be there, but two of the comics anthologies I'm in will be: Comics Blogger's Death Spark 2 and Sean Knickerbocker's Rust Belt Review Vol 6.
