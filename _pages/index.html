I'm excited to share my first self-published graphic novel with you! 6+ years in the making and topping off at nearly 300 pages, Chrysanthemum Under the Waves is a collection of nine comics. It's a book of mourning, a book of letting go, a book of love and loss.

You can preorder now. Advanced copies will be available at CXC (Cartoon Crossroads Columbus) 9/26-9/29/2024 and on the fall leg of my Less is More Zine Tour.
