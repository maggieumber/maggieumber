There are 78 cards in a Tarot Deck. The deck is divided into the Major Arcana (22 cards) and the Minor Arcana (56 cards). The Minor Arcana is further divided into Wands, Pentacles, Cups and Swords. Each of the suites in the Minor Arcana contains 14 cards.

The most widely used Tarot Card deck is the Rider-Waite-Smith deck. The Rider Company was the deck's first publisher in 1909. A. E. Waite was the deck's mystic advisor. The deck's illustrator, whose credit is sometimes left out of the name, was Pamela Colman Smith.
