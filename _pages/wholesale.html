Hello retailers! Thank you for carrying my books in your shop. To qualify for wholesale you must order at least 10 copies. 10 copies of 1 title, or 10 copies spread out over multiple titles. If you do not meet these terms your order will be refunded.

Shipping is free for wholesale orders placed in the USA!

International wholesale orders will have shipping costs added after you've placed your order. I'll contact you directly with an invoice to cover the shipping.

Any questions? Please contact me via ✍ email.
